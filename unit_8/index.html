About

Obstacles races are not just for adventure seekers, they are for anyone on a path to better fitness and to greater mental and physical strength. Learning to face your fears on the race course will help you conquer any obstacle that life puts in your path. Join me on the path to learning to live fearless.

Train

Obstacle course races challenge you to train differently than other sports. You must train for endurance, strenghth, and agility. It also requires mental toughness. To learn more about overall training and training for specific obstacles, click here.

Nourish

You are what you eat. To be a beast, you must eat like a beast. While I do not believe that one size fits all, I do believe we perform optimally when we fuel our bodies with whole foods. I personally try to cook using primal, paleo, and clean recipes. Click here to find links to meal ideas, prep shortcuts, and articles on nutrition to fuel your body.
