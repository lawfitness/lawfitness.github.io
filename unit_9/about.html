About

Obstacles races are not just for adventure seekers, they are for anyone on a path to better fitness and to greater mental and physical strength. Learning to face your fears on the race course will help you conquer any obstacle that life puts in your path. Join me on the path to learning to live fearless.

About Me

By day, I'm a research editor. The rest of the time? I'm a superhero in training. No, I am not a professional athlete. I'm just a woman on a mission to tackle anything that life throws at her. This site does not have all the answers; it is my goal to help like-minded people get off the couch, get out of a rut, and get over the walls that hold them back.
